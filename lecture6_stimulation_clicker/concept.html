Stimulation Clicker

Layout Experiments Final Concept

Idea Summary

My worst website idea is a stimulation clicker page (Heavy inspiration from Neal Agarwal.). The final version would have a bar that rises when the user clicks and slowly falls when the user stops. As the bar gets higher, the website would add more distractions and make it harder to continue clicking.

I would exaggerate repetition by repeating notifications, buttons, warnings, and other attention-grabbing elements across the page. The experience would begin simple, then become increasingly crowded and annoying as the user makes progress.

How This Lecture Supports the Idea

Borders, padding, and margins can shape the progress bar and click area. Floats can place fake advertisements on both sides of the page. Relative and absolute positioning can move pop-ups over the click area and make the page feel more difficult to use.
